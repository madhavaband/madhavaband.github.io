About Madhava

Madhava is a musical duo from NYC. Ryan D. Lee and Madhava Hansen met in 2011 while helping move a mutual friends piano in Queens. They played in a band together for about a year in 2012. Madhava decided to record an acoustic record at Ryan's home studio in 2015. What started out as an acoustic record turned into a full on collaboration between them and led to the duo Madhava being formed.
