About Madhava

Hailing from the cityscapes of New York, Alt/Rock recording artist Madhava began hitting the ground running in the music industry as a solo act this year in 2017, and holds a lot of promise as he continues to manifest his multifaceted signature sound and active career. Madhava gained a lot of his experience in the New York City music scene while playing in bands at some of the city's most prominent venues, such as: The Knitting Factory, Mercury Lounge, Rockwood Music Hall, The Bitter End, and many more. On October 13th of this year in 2017, Madhava released his debut album "User", which was produced by Ryan D. Lee at Penguin House Studios in Williamsburg, Brooklyn; mixed by Raul Abellan at Millenia Estudios in Valencia, Spain; and mastered by 5-time Latin Grammy Award winner Alfonso Rodenas in Los Angeles. A collective and elite effort between the years of 2015 and 2017. As Madhava continues to move forward as a solo artist with a very promising 2018 on the horizon, be sure to connect with him through his official Social Media and Music Streaming platforms for News, Music, Shows, Events and Booking.
